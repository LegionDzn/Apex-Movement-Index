Binds

Some of the fundamental movement tech in Apex Legends relies on rapid button inputs, to aid in consistency and ease of execution of this tech it is advised that you set both Move Forward and Jump to scroll wheel directions. ex move forward to scroll up and jump to scroll down. It doesn't matter which scroll direction either is bound to, but I've found that scroll down bound to jump and scroll up bound to forward works better for me than the other way around.
