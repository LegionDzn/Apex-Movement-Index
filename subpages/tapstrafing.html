Tapstrafing

Changing Direction With Tap Strafe

Tapstrafing is tech that allows you to sharply turn mid-air without losing much momentum, you can think of it as a supercharged air strafe. A Tapstrafe can be performed by spamming your forward input (typically by scrolling) and sharply air strafing in the direction you want to go all while mid-air.
Note that when you exit a tapstrafe your direction will be offset by 45 degrees in the direction you tapstrafed in. For example, if you want to tapstrafe 180 degrees you'd end your tapstrafe at 135 degrees which would result in an overall 180 degrees. (135+45=180)
