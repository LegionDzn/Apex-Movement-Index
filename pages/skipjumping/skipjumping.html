Skip Jumping Methods

A Skipjump allows you to get a full momentum slide while skipping the slide animation. To perform a skip jump you must input a CROUCH and a JUMP at the same time, between 200 and 220 velocity all while holding a weapon. there are a few ways to get ourselves within the velocity margin, as shown in the video.

How to perform each skip jump in the order they're shown.

1. Begin sprinting from a walk, once you are within the velocity margin input CROUCH + JUMP.

2. Once sprinting, hold sprint, while sprint is held briefly ads slowing yourself down to less than 200 velocity, once you're within the velocity margin, input CROUCH + JUMP.

3. While at full sprint, strafe with either LEFT or RIGHT to slow yourself, once you're within the velocity margin, input CROUCH + JUMP.

4. While at full sprint, sharply move your camera, this will slow you down, once you're within the velocity margin, input CROUCH + JUMP. The amount of speed loss is proportional to the angle and speed you moved your camera, so theoretically you could land within the margin and skip jump instantly.

5. This method is similar to #4, but in this case, you move your camera a full 360 degrees to slow yourself.
