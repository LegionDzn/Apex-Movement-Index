Mantle Skips

Momentum Shift Mantle Skip

Momentum Shift Mantle Skipping can be done by performing a walljump then shortly after inputing AWD/DWA, your Movement should shift towards the wall thereby pushing you over the wall. If you are experiencing a very short mantel animation after pressing AWD/DWA ensure that you are pressing AWD/DWA shortly after the walljump and not while you are on the wall.

Tapstrafe Mantle Skip

This is a basic walljump + a tapstrafe to redirect ourselves over the wall. This is the more quick and dirty method of mantel skipping than AWD/DWA skipping due to you being able to approach the wall at nearly any angle.

Fatuige Wall Jump Mantle Skip

A fatuige mantel skip combined with the redirect from section A. This method leaves you the most vlunable during a gunfight and is also harder to pull off, but can be useful if you are already jump fatigued.
