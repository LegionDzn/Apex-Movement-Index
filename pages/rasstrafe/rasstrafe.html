Ras Strafing

Ras strafing is essentially a supercharged momentum shift, as a global rule, you can ras strafe by tapstrafing while pressing the last two inputs in the input order. So as in this video, W>A>>S>D

In the video the input order goes as such: 1.W>A>(Start Tapstrafing)>S>D 2.W>D>(Start Tapstrafing)>S>A

You can also see that I move my camera a bit during the ras strafe, this is to make the tapstrafe more powerful, thereby allowing a tighter ras strafe.

Fatuge Wall Jumping + Ras Strafe

A ras strafe can be done out of a fatigue wall bounce, allowing for a creative and unexpected peek. A ras strafe can also be done out of a standard wall jump (as seen in the latter clip), but I don't find it as useful due to the need for a run-up.
