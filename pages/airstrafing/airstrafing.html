Air Strafe

Airstrafing exists in apex as it does in many other source games, but is somewhat nerfed when compared to other source engine games like CS: GO or Team Fortress 2. For a more clear example, you can look at CS surf gameplay, where it's the primary method of in-air navigation.

Althoguh it exists in a somehwat nerfed state when compared to other source engine games like CS: GO or Team Fortress 2, it's still used quite frequrntly when jumping off high ledges or when using Pathfinder's grapple.

To airstrafe simply press the directional input in the direction you are moving your camera. e.g. Holding LEFT and moving your mouse Left.
