Fall Stun Cancel

Wall Touch Method

The Wall Touch Method of Fall Stun canceling is performed by touching a wall before hitting the ground. This works due to the fact that you don't incur fall stun when in the climbing state, so by putting yourself in the climbing state while you hit the ground, you don't incur fall stun.
